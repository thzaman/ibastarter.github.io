*There is a pass-bar for every MCQ sub and Descriptive pt as well.
*Descriptive copy will only be checked once pass-bar in all 3 MCQ subs is attained.
*Top 160 scoring examinees among those who passed all 3 MCQ subs and Descriptive will be called up for Viva.
*The written exam held in viva day's morning doesn't carry any marks but is imperative to attend to ensure your attendance.
*Around top 120 people totalled by MCQ, Descriptive and Viva marks will be finally selected.
*There'll be no waiting list. If anyone chooses not to get admitted, their seat will be kept empty. So it's advised to consider properly if called for viva.
